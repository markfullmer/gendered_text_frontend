Emily Parkhurst

Emily Parkhurst is a geek, gamer, dungeon master, genetic counselor, researcher and an avid reader of all genres, including fanfic. In the past she has been a Sunday school teacher, cellist, lifeguard, LGBT peer counselor, musical theater usher, pregnancy clinic counselor, and research lab intern (but not all at the same time).

She started doing triathlons after college and has completed over fifteen Olympic distance triathlons. She a begrudging runner and has done a few half-marathons. However, her favorite race is still the two mile Dwight Crum Pier-to-Pier ocean swim every summer.
